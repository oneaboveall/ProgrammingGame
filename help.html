So, you're not as tough as you thought you were eh? Well...Welcome to the help window!

Some things to know...

  • Click on buttons to move around and make descisions!
  • You cannot start your story until you pick a name...Everyone needs a name.
  • Some events are time sensitive, so pay attention!
  • There are a total of 12 places where you can die. Can you find them all?
  • Hopefully you have some sense of spatial, the moves are made in absolute directions, and a few relative ones.
  • Those pesky map, compas and cake might be around here somewhere...or they might not be around at all.
  • There can be empty rooms or dead ends, what fun is a dungeon thats linear?
  • Orks provide a chance to gain more experience (Attack Power), so don't pass up a chance to kill them!
  • When you've found everything you might be entered in a chance to win a fantastic, one-of-a-kind prize! No purchase nessecary, details on reverse side of page.
  • Many brave adventurers have come before you...how many will come after you?
